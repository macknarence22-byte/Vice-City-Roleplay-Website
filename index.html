What is Vice City?

Vice City Roleplay is a multi-platform Global Roleplay Network. Our community spans multiple platforms so players can experience Vice City in different ways depending on how they play.

Platforms

FiveM

Our FiveM server provides a Vice City experience with custom scripts, immersive roleplay systems.

Xbox

Vice City Xbox roleplay focuses on immersive console RP with organized departments, community events, and player driven stories.

PlayStation 5

The PS5 Vice City community delivers structured console roleplay with factions, police departments, and organized community events.

The Vice City Global

Vice City is more than a server it's a Global Community. Whether you play on PC or console, you can take part in the Vice City Roleplay and help shape the stories that unfold within it.
