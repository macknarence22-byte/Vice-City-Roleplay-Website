Vice City

A Multi-Platform Roleplay Community

What is Vice City?

Vice City is a multi-platform roleplay network inspired by the neon streets, crime culture, and nightlife of a fictional coastal metropolis. Our community spans multiple platforms so players can experience Vice City in different ways depending on how they play.

Platforms

FiveM

Our FiveM server provides the most advanced Vice City experience with custom scripts, immersive roleplay systems, player-owned businesses, and a fully living city.

Xbox

Vice City Xbox roleplay focuses on immersive console RP with organized departments, community events, and player driven stories.

PlayStation 5

The PS5 Vice City community delivers structured console roleplay with factions, police departments, and organized community events.

The Vice City Network

Vice City is more than a server — it's a connected community spanning multiple platforms. Whether you play on PC or console, you can take part in the Vice City world and help shape the stories that unfold within it.
